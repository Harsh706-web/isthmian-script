ABOUT THIS DICTIONARY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Source: Dictionnaire de la langue nahuatl classique by Alexis Wimmer
Based on:
- Rémi Simon's 1885 French translation of Molina's 1571 Vocabulario
- The Florentine Codex (Codex de Florence) by Fray Bernardino de Sahagún

Nahuatl long vowels are marked with macrons: ā, ē, ī, ō, ū
The dictionary includes botanical, zoological, historical, and cultural terms.
